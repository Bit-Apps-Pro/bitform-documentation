Installation, Activate and Upgrade of Bitform

In this section we will show you how you can install Bit Form on your WordPress site & also how you can activate Bit Form pro version.

Process of installation & activation#

Follow the path mentioned below:

  1. At first, navigate to your wordpress & navigate to plugin. Then click on "Add new".
  2. Go to search option & search 'Bit Form'.





  1. Click "Install now" & then "Activate" free version.



  1. Now you can see Bit Form in your WordPress plugin list.



  1. After successful installation you can see the home page of 'Bit Form'. Now You are ready to create form.

Process of activating premium version :#

  1. At first go to our website bitpress.pro
  2. Go to 'Pricing' section of pro version.



  1. Now you can see all the plans of Bit Form.
  2. Select your desired plan & you can customize the quantity of sites.
  3. Click on 'Proceed'.
  4. You have to fill up the form for membership account to use the premium version of Bit Form.



  1. Give your location & payment information.



  1. Pay the amount via any credit card or PayPal. Also can use coupon card if you have any.





  1. After you will be redirected to subscription page.
  2. You can check your subscription is activated or not by reloading the page.



  1. After you will be notified via your email with download link , license key, tutorial link & documentation link.



  1. Now, go to your subscription dashboard. Here you can see your subscription details. You can also update your plans at any time.



  1. Download the pro version by clicking the option.
  2. Then go to your dashboard & again navigate to plugin option.
  3. Click on 'Add New' & select 'upload plugin'.





  1. Click on ' Choose File' & select the zip file you have downloaded before. Then click on 'Install Now'.



  1. Then press on 'Activate Plugin'. After you will see the pro version of Bit Form on your plugin list.





  1. Then go to 'Bit Form' from dashboard & you will find license option. And now copy the key from subscription page & paste the key on license key option.



  1. After clicking 'Submit' button you will be shown a successful message.

Congratulations! Now you are a pro member of the Bit Form. Now you can access pro features, updates & support.
