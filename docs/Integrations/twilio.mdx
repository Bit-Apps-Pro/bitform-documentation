## Authorization Process
1. At first, select the form you want to apply the Twilio integration. If you don’t have any then create a new one from **Create Form**.  After that, follow the below instructions. Follow the path:

  ***Settings => Integrations =>  Click on (+) => Click on ‘Twilio’***.

 <Image src="twilio/create-form" alt="Twilio" w="1020" h="300" />

 <Image src="twilio/settings-to-integration" alt="Twilio" w="1020" h="250" />

 <Image src="twilio/select-twilio" alt="Twilio" w="1020" h="300" />

2.	**Integration Name:** Set the proper integration name. Default it's taken **Twilio**.
 <Image src="twilio/set-integration-name" alt="Twilio" w="1020" h="270" />

### Integration Settings
1.	After that, you have to copy the Account SID, Auth Token, and from (Phone Number) from your Twilio account & paste on the field.     
**To get the Account SID, Auth Token, and from (Phone Number),**   
- click on `Twilio Console` or go to your Twilio console dashboard 
- click on Account => API Keys & Tokens
-	Now copy the *Account SID*, *Auth Token* and paste it into the integration’s dashboard.
-	Enter your twilio phone number From (Phone Number) field
-	Click on **`“Authorize”`** button

 <Image src="twilio/api-console-click" alt="Twilio" w="1020" h="300" />
 <Image src="twilio/copy-api" alt="Twilio" w="1020" h="300" />
 <Image src="twilio/authorize" alt="Twilio" w="1020" h="300" />

2.	Click on `Next` to set up the other integration settings ahead.

3.	After completing the authorization process, you will find the Map Field option. You have to map **To (Phone Number)**, and **Message** fields with the related Twilio field.  Now, click on the `Next` button to move forward for further settings. 
 <Image src="twilio/map-fields" alt="Twilio" w="1020" h="300" />

:::info
 If your Twilio account is free, you must verify the receiver's phone number. Otherwise, the integration will not work. [Click to verify the receiver's phone number](https://console.twilio.com/us1/develop/phone-numbers/manage/verified).
:::

4.	Save the integration by clicking the **Finish & Save** Button. The Twilio integration is ready.

 <Image src="twilio/finish-and-Save" alt="Twilio" w="1020" h="250" />

 <Image src="twilio/twilio-integration" alt="Twilio" w="1020" h="300" />

5.	Now, you have to set conditional logic to trigger the integration for the submitted data. When you set conditional logic, remember some points – **set Action behavior** = **Always & specify** the integration name you have **created** for Twilio. If you don’t select the integration name properly, then the integration will not work.

 <Image src="twilio/conditional-logic" alt="Twilio" w="1020" h="300" />

6. Now, whenever a user will submit the form it will be added to the corresponding Contact list of Twilio. Also can view from form responses of Bit Form.

 <Image src="twilio/twilio-list" alt="Twilio" w="1020" h="270" />
 
 <Image src="twilio/responses" alt="Twilio" w="1020" h="250" />

7. You can view every success API of the submitted data from the timeline of related info.

 <Image src="twilio/api-response" alt="Twilio" w="1020" h="390" />
