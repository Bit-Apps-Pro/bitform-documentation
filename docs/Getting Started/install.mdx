In this section we will show you how you can install Bit Form on your WordPress site & also how you can activate Bit Form pro version.

## Process of installation & activation

Follow the path mentioned below:

1. At first, navigate to your wordpress  & navigate to plugin. Then click on **"Add new"**.
2. Go to search option & search **'Bit Form'**.

# Process of activating premium version :

1. At first go to our website bitpress.pro
2. Go to '**Pricing**' section of pro version. 
![Go to pricing plan-Bit Form](https://github.com/Bit-Press/bitform-documentation/blob/main/static/img/Get%20Started.png?raw=true)
3. Now you can see all the plans of Bit Form.
4. Select your desired plan & you can customize the quantity of sites.
5. Click on '**Proceed**'.
6. You have to fill up the form for **membership account** to use the premium version of Bit Form.
![Create membership account-Bit Form](https://github.com/Bit-Press/bitform-documentation/blob/main/static/img/Membeship%20Account.png?raw=true)
7. Give your **location** & **payment information**.
![Set location & payment information-Bit Form](https://github.com/Bit-Press/bitform-documentation/blob/main/static/img/Location%20&%20Payment.png?raw=true)
8. Pay the amount via any **credit card** or **PayPal**. Also can use **coupon card** if you have any.
![Pay via credit card & PayPal-Bit Form](https://github.com/Bit-Press/bitform-documentation/blob/main/static/img/PayPal.png?raw=true)
![Add Cupon-Bit Form](https://github.com/Bit-Press/bitform-documentation/blob/main/static/img/Add%20coupon.png?raw=true)
9. After you will be redirected to **subscription page.**
10. You can check your subscription is activated or not by reloading the page. 
![Subscription Page-Bit Form](https://github.com/Bit-Press/bitform-documentation/blob/main/static/img/Suscription%20Page.png?raw=true)
11. After you will be notified via your email with **download link** , **license key**, **tutorial link** & **documentation link**.
![Confirmation mail-Bit Form](https://github.com/Bit-Press/bitform-documentation/blob/main/static/img/Welcome%20email.png?raw=true)
12. Now, go to your subscription dashboard. Here you can see your subscription details. You can also update your plans at any time.
![Subscription details-Bit Form](https://github.com/Bit-Press/bitform-documentation/blob/main/static/img/Subscription%20details.png?raw=true)
13. Download the pro version by clicking the option.
14. Then go to your dashboard & again navigate to plugin option.
15. Click on '**Add New**' & select '**upload plugin'**.
![Upload Bit Form pro-Bit Form](https://github.com/Bit-Press/bitform-documentation/blob/main/static/img/Upload%20Plugin.png?raw=true)
![Upload Bit Form pro-Bit Form](https://github.com/Bit-Press/bitform-documentation/blob/main/static/img/Upload%20Plugin2.png?raw=true)
16. Click on ' **Choose File**' & select the zip file you have downloaded before. Then click on '**Install Now**'.
![Install Bit Form pro-Bit Form](https://github.com/Bit-Press/bitform-documentation/blob/main/static/img/Choose%20&%20install.png?raw=true)
17. Then press on '**Activate Plugin'**. After you will see the pro version of Bit Form on your plugin list.
![Activate plugin-Bit Form](https://github.com/Bit-Press/bitform-documentation/blob/main/static/img/Activate%20Plugin.png?raw=true)
![Successful activation-Bit Form](https://github.com/Bit-Press/bitform-documentation/blob/main/static/img/Successfully%20activated.png?raw=true)
![View Bit Form pro on plugin list-Bit Form](https://github.com/Bit-Press/bitform-documentation/blob/main/static/img/Pro%20in%20dashboard.png?raw=true)
18. Then go to **'Bit Form'** from dashboard & you will find license option. And now copy the key from subscription page & paste the key on license key option.
![Paste license key-Bit Form](https://github.com/Bit-Press/bitform-documentation/blob/main/static/img/License%20.png?raw=true)
19. After clicking '**Submit**' button you will be shown a successful message.
